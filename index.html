About Edu-Rays

A Global Tuition & Holistic Learning Centre Based in Vizag

Edu-Rays is a growing educational institution that combines personalized academic coaching with a wide range of holistic development programs. Founded by a passionate educator, Edu-Rays has steadily expanded from its base in Visakhapatnam (Vizag), India, to teaching students across India, Singapore, Australia, and other international regions.

With its strong commitment to individualized attention, structured learning, and all-round development, Edu-Rays has become a trusted destination for families seeking high-quality education.
